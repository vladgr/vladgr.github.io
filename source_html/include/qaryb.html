Description.

Carpooling and marketplace app for buyers and sellers for Arabic region.


  • Flutter Map integration.
  • Geo location.
  • Camera.
  • Permissions.
  • Chat and web sockets.
  • Push notifications (Firebase integration).
  • Multiple languages: English, French and Arabic.
  • RTL design for Arabic version.
  • Dependency Injection.
  • Sentry for logging.
  • REST API.
  • MobX for state management.
